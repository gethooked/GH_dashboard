Prep and Production

Main Shares

Plan for fish buying and species assignment. Shows amounts needed for main share upgrades with interactive controls to test assignment scenarios.

SpeciesAssignment

Assign species and download email list, new members list, and main share labels.

Product Key

Create a product key after new products are added.

Early Deadline Orders

View tallies of early deadline products ordered and download orders for vendors and labels for preordered products. See guide here.

Special Orders

View total weight of products ordered and download special order labels. See guide here.

Home Delivery

Export a Routesavvy-friendly list of addresses and generate routes with product and delivery notes..

Checklists

Download pickup site cooler checklists for packing in the kitchen and taking to sites.

Marketing

Weekly Reminder Email List

Export a list of people to email tagged with their schedules ('onweek' or 'offweek').

Data

Weekly Archive

Download lists and summarizes of deliveries, sales, spending by customer, and flashsale charge amounts from current production week.

Inactive Weekly Archive

Perform weekly archive tasks and see summaries for a production week that has been archived (Data is no longer in Active Deliveries.) See instructions in app for details.

Monthly Bag Counts

Calculate customers' bag balances and download a list of customers to email and charge for unreturned bags.
